Our Story

The Finest Multicuisine Legacy

Established as a premium culinary destination along Gangapur Road, River Dine Restaurant & Banquet has defined high-class dining in Nashik. We combine authentic North Indian recipes, charred clay-tandoor delicacies, global Chinese woks, and continental classics under one roof.

Perfectly situated opposite the iconic Asaram Bapu Bridge, River Dine offers a dual experience: a serene garden-deck dining area with tranquil open-air vibes, and a spacious luxury banquet hall designed to host family celebrations, engagements, and corporate banquets of 100 to 150 guests.

Uniting fresh local spices, premium ingredients, and a beautiful river-bridge view to create unforgettable dining memories.

Vikram Salve

Executive Chef & Banquet Supervisor

Chef Vikram Salve leads the kitchen at River Dine, bringing over 15 years of rich experience in authentic North Indian, Tandoori, and Maharashtrian cuisines. Having catered for large-scale events across Mumbai and Pune, he excels in crafting consistent, flavourful banqueting menus.

At River Dine, we take pride in serving high-quality multicuisine food. Whether you are dining as a couple on our garden deck or hosting a wedding for 150 guests in our banquet hall, each dish is cooked to perfection.
